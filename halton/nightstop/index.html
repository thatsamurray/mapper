Nightstop Communities North West CIC

Provides emergency accommodation for vulnerable young people and community mental health and wellbeing services

Support available

Wellbeing Support

1-1 Counselling,Anger Management, Group/Family Therapy, Mediation, Online Counselling, Advice Service and Support Groups “Ladies First” & “Funda-MEN-tals”

Emergency Accommodation

Safe and Secure Emergency Accommodation is available for people aged 16-35yrs old, who are homeless or at risk of becoming homeless.

Emergency Host Accommodation

From 1 night to 6 weeks for young people aged 16-25yrs who are at risk of homelessness or who are experiencing homelessness.

Supported Lodgings

For vulnerable young people (16-18yrs) living in host accommodation receiving support with life skills.

Supported Housing

Temporary accommodation for vulnerable people (18-35yrs), living semi independently.
